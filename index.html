About Us

Our platform is designed to analyze customer sentiment towards popular online tutoring applications in Indonesia, such as Ruangguru, Pahamify, Quipper, and Cerebrum. By combining machine learning technology and data visualization, we provide accurate and easy-to-understand information to help users make informed decisions.

Our research is based on collecting data from social media, where real customer opinions are gathered and processed into actionable analysis. Our goal is to provide transparency and support the growth of the edtech sector in Indonesia, while helping students, parents, and educators choose the best application to meet their needs.
