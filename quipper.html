About Quipper

Quipper is an educational technology company dedicated to providing quality education, even in remote areas. Founded in 2010 in London, UK, Quipper's services are now available in several countries including Japan, the Philippines, and Indonesia. Since 2015, Quipper has been part of Indonesia's education ecosystem, improving and distributing high-quality educational resources for both teachers and students.

Quipper offers services such as Quipper School for learning management, Quipper School Premium for remote learning, as well as video-based e-learning and a campus information portal for students in Indonesia.
