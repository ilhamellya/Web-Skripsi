About Pahamify

Pahamify is a leading educational technology company in Indonesia, known for its engaging learning app that offers thousands of premium animated videos, quizzes, and summaries. Combining learning science, filmmaking, gamification, and intuitive design, Pahamify helps students enhance their learning experience. The company focuses not only on academics but also on character development and career progression.

Founded in 2018 by Fikri and Ikhsan, Pahamify began as the YouTube channel Hujan Tanda Tanya. Its mission is to enhance student performance through interactive learning, test preparation, and live tutoring. With its innovative Pahamify Framework, the company has reached all 34 provinces in Indonesia and helped many students gain admission to top universities.
